What makes it different

Phi Tree Möbius Synth is an experimental instrument for sounds that grow, fold and transform while you play. Its mathematically inspired voice system moves between familiar musical harmony and more unusual spectral colour, producing tones that feel organic, animated and difficult to achieve with conventional subtractive synthesis. It can create delicate ambient voices, cinematic drones, metallic leads, unstable textures and expressive playable sounds without requiring the musician to understand the mathematics behind it.

Perfect for

  • Ambient composition
  • Cinematic drones
  • Expressive leads
  • Experimental timbres

Key features

  • A distinctive synthesis concept inspired by golden-ratio relationships
  • Möbius-inspired motion that makes tones fold, drift and reconnect
  • Continuous movement between harmonic and more unfamiliar tonal colours
  • Monophonic and polyphonic performance modes
  • Expressive response to velocity and aftertouch
  • Assignable modulation for animated, evolving performances
  • Integrated drift, FM, delay, feedback, reverb and output shaping
