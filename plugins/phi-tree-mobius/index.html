What makes it different

Phi Tree Möbius Synth builds its voice architecture around golden-ratio relationships, a seven-oscillator phi tree and Möbius-inspired movement. The result is an instrument designed for harmonic structures that feel ordered yet continuously in motion.

Perfect for

  • Evolving pads
  • Experimental harmony
  • Ambient composition
  • Expressive synthesis

Key features

  • Seven-oscillator phi-tree architecture
  • Golden-ratio and Fibonacci-inspired relationships
  • Möbius-style motion and evolving modulation
  • Polyphonic MIDI instrument
  • Amplitude shaping and performance controls
  • Built-in delay and reverb
  • macOS and Windows downloads

Creative use

A deeply expressive synthesizer shaped by golden-ratio harmony, Möbius-inspired motion and continuously evolving timbre. Use it in DAWs, live setups, installations, experimental composition, ambient music, electronic production and sound-design workflows.
