What makes it different

ImageSonicFlux creates a direct relationship between visual form and sound movement. Load an image, follow a visible contour or draw your own path, and the plugin turns that geometry into an animated trajectory for the audio arriving from your DAW. Movement can be smooth and atmospheric, rhythmic and repetitive, or highly experimental, making the plugin equally suited to electronic production, installation work, cinematic sound and spatial composition.

Perfect for

  • Spatial composition
  • Installation art
  • Stereo movement
  • Multi-speaker performance

Key features

  • Image-driven sound movement with a workflow unlike conventional automation
  • Use visible image contours as spatial trajectories
  • Draw, edit, save and reload your own custom motion paths
  • Loop and ping-pong traversal styles
  • Stereo, binaural and multi-output spatial movement
  • Control over speed, width, depth, smoothing and speaker spread
  • Designed for musicians, composers, installation artists and sound designers
