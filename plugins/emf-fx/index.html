What makes it different

Earth Magnetic Field FX transforms incoming audio through movement inspired by the changing magnetic activity of the planet. Instead of relying only on predictable LFO cycles, it uses scientific data as a source of evolving behaviour, giving reverb, pitch movement, reverse textures and stereo space a more organic and constantly shifting character. The result can range from subtle atmospheric animation to dramatic cinematic transformation and extreme experimental processing.

Perfect for

  • Ambient music
  • Film scoring
  • Experimental production
  • Spatial sound design

Key features

  • Scientific geomagnetic activity used as a source of sonic movement
  • Evolving spatial reverb and stereo animation
  • Aurora-inspired pitch echoes and atmospheric trails
  • Reverse-delay textures with organic variation
  • Multiple interpretation modes for different styles of movement
  • Adjustable data speed, intensity and spatial behaviour
  • Freeze and extreme processing options for sustained or unstable textures
