What makes it different

HeisenKaon combines several destructive and reconstructive effect modules inside one performance-oriented laboratory. Granular processing, buffer manipulation, bit reduction, resonance and unstable delay behaviours can be combined without forcing the sound into a predictable result.

Perfect for

  • Glitch production
  • Sound destruction
  • Experimental performance
  • Texture design

Key features

  • Granular processing
  • Buffer scrub and fragmentation
  • Bitcrusher and ring modulation
  • Delay abuse and resonant-body modules
  • Module-specific modulation
  • Performance-oriented mix controls
  • macOS and Windows downloads

Creative use

A sound-destruction laboratory for glitch, fragmentation, resonance and controlled sonic instability. Use it in DAWs, live setups, installations, experimental composition, ambient music, electronic production and sound-design workflows.
