What makes it different

HeisenKaon is designed for producers who want to push sound beyond conventional processing. It can fracture rhythms, reorganise textures, create unstable echoes, generate resonant movement and turn clean material into complex evolving sound. Effects can be explored individually or combined into a remembered multi-effect chain, while modulation and automated mixing turn static settings into living systems that continue changing over time.

Perfect for

  • Glitch design
  • Texture destruction
  • Transitions and risers
  • Experimental processing

Key features

  • Six contrasting processors for digital fracture, granular motion and resonance
  • Bitcrushing, granular, buffer, ring-modulation, delay and resonant treatments
  • Single-effect workflow or custom Multi-FX Chain mode
  • The plugin remembers your assembled effect chain
  • Independent mix control for every active processor
  • Automated effect-mix movement and assignable modulation
  • Post-effect oscilloscope for immediate visual feedback
