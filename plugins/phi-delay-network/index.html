What makes it different

Phi Delay Network replaces a single conventional delay line with seven related delay environments. Phi and Fibonacci-inspired timing relationships create echoes that evolve, overlap and resist obvious repetition.

Perfect for

  • Evolving echoes
  • Ambient production
  • Rhythmic delay
  • Experimental mixing

Key features

  • Seven distinct delay modes
  • Phi and Fibonacci-inspired timing structures
  • Serial and parallel routing
  • Per-mode feedback behaviour
  • Wet/dry and direction controls
  • MIDI and test-oscillator support
  • macOS and Windows downloads

Creative use

Seven original delay environments inspired by phi, Fibonacci growth and non-repeating motion. Use it in DAWs, live setups, installations, experimental composition, ambient music, electronic production and sound-design workflows.
